About me


Hello there!

My name is Hugo Mellbris and I study UX design at the Stockholm Institute of Technology. For the past ten years, I have been worked in the restaurant industry, mostly as a bartender. During my time behind the bar, I have always been interested in designs of various kinds. I was recommended to check out UX, and I was quickly drawn to the profession.

My interests revolve mainly around gaming, training, and football. If I do not sit in front of the computer or run on the treadmill, I like to travel around the world and discover new places. In the future, I’d like to work in the gaming industry or on environmental projects.

If you want to know more about me, do not hesitate to get in touch!

Well met, Hugo
